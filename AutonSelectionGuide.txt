Digital Ports: 
Port 1: If the pin is in, the robot will use Arcade Mode, if it is not, the robot will use Tank Mode
Port 2: Nothing
Port 3: Auton Selection 1
Port 4: Auton Selection 2
Port 5: Auton Selection 3
Port 6-12: Nothing

Auton Selection:

For simple auton: No pins
For Blue Flag auton: AS1 ON
For Other Blue auton: AS2 ON
For Red Flag auton: AS1 AND AS2 ON
For Other Red auton: AS3 ON
